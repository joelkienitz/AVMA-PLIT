our brand story
In 2014, we embarked on a comprehensive research study to understand the needs of current AVMA PLIT members. Everything we learned from our members culminated in the new AVMA PLIT: Protecting you through it all initiative. And it’s more than simply a new look. This exciting new initiative will ultimately enhance our members’ understanding of the insurance solutions and resources through comprehensive tools and educational programs that help veterinarians minimize their risk of facing claims and help them better understand their business needs.

"For more than 50 years we’ve helped protect the reputations of veterinarians at all stages of their careers and helped guide them through tough legal situations and license complaints. AVMA members continue to tell us that the PLIT program is one of the most valuable components of their AVMA membership; we want to build on our success and ensure that our colleagues are aware of the full scope of products and services—from professional liability to business and personal insurance."

Janet Donlin, DVM, AVMA PLIT CEO
